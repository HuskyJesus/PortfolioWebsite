Caius Ruscella

Hi, I'm Caius - a narrative designer and game developer focused on creating meaningful, immersive, and interactive experiences. I specialize in blending technical Unity/Unreal development with story-first design thinking.

Currently pursuing my MS in Interactive Entertainment at the Florida Interactive Entertainment Academy while serving as Design Lead on Mythbound at Skaldforge, where I own systems design, GDD documentation, and narrative production.

My background spans VR/AR/XR development, 360° cinematic production, and client-facing studio work across AccessVR, VOTU LLC, SCIL Labs, and my own studio, Ruscella Immersive LLC.
