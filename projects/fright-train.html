Fright Train

Solo Project  ·  Unity / C#  ·  Horror

A horror game built in Unity that subverts the cheerful world of Thomas the Tank Engine. Players navigate a tense environment filled with note interactions, scripted triggers, and progression systems designed to build dread through environmental storytelling rather than jump scares.

What I Built

  • Environment asset implementation
  • Gameplay trigger scripting
  • Note interactions
  • Progression systems to support environmental storytelling
